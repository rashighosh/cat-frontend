You will be able to browse 3 pre-selected clinical trials.

On the left side of your screen, you will see 3 pre-selected clinical trials. Click the study number buttons at the bottom to navigate between the 3 clinical trials.



You can ask Alex questions about the clinical trials.

On the right side of your screen, you can chat with Alex about the trials. You need to refer to each trial by their study number (eg, Study 1) so Alex knows which study you are asking about.

Alex cannot help you enroll in any of the studies, but you will be able to request more information about a study after talking to Alex.



Ask as many or as little questions as you'd like!

There are no requirements for how many questions you have to ask Alex. When you're done, click the button in the bottom right to finish the virtual interaction.
